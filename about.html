About Nerdtritionists

We are a team of two people, Mr. and Mrs. Nerdtritionist.

With our youtube channel, we try to help people to make better dietary choices. Our goal is to provide the necessary education to live a healthy lifestyle and make informed decisions about their nutrition.

Mr. Nerdtritionist

Mr. Nerdtritionist writes the manuscript of the videos and is known as the voice of the channel. Apart from that, he creates the thumbnails and does the SEO. He is responsible for the presence on Facebook and Instagram.

Mrs. Nerdtritionist

Mrs. Nerdtritionist works as a certified nutritionist and reviews the manuscript of the videos. She is responsible for the editing of the videos and for the presence on TikTok.

Infrequently asked questions

  • Are you vegan?

    Yes, both Mr. and Mrs. Nerdtritionist eat a whole food plant-based diet.

  • What do you do apart from your youtube channel?

    Mrs. Nerdtritionist works as a dietitian. Mr. Nerdtritionist works as a backend-developer.
    Both like to play video games in their free time.

  • Which books about nutrition can you recommend?

    We highly recommend "How not to die" and "How not to diet" by Dr. Michael Greger.
    If you are hooked on protein, we recommend reading "Proteinaholics" by Dr. Garth Davis.
